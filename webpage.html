The Solution: A Hardware-based Trust Platform

"Project Nalam" is not another app. It's a unified, solar-powered hardware platform that functions as a **trusted "Hardware Oracle"** for governance. It is the immutable, verifiable link between the citizen, their digital identity, and the state, built on four modular pillars.

1. "Jan-Setu" (The People's Bridge)

The Resilient Connectivity Core, using Satellite + 4G/5G and deploying a village-wide LoRaWAN IoT fabric.

2. "Thevai Munaiyam" (The Need Terminal)

An inclusive, Bhashini-powered voice-first AI portal for all grievances and information, providing signed, trackable receipts.

3. "Sevai Jannal" (The Service Window)

A "Hardware Oracle" for state blockchains, verifying documents from "Physical-to-Chain" and "Chain-to-Physical."

4. "Dhan Seva" (The Wealth Service)

A biometrically-secured hardware wallet for digital welfare (e-RUPI) with an integrated AePS Micro-ATM for "leakage-proof" redemption.
